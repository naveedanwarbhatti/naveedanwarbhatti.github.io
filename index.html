Welcome to my personal corner of the internet! I'm an innovative and passionate Assistant Professor at LUMS in Lahore, Pakistan, where I endeavor to inspire the next generation of tech visionaries. Prior to this, I had the privilege of being an ERCIM Alain Bensoussan Post-Doctoral Fellow, working with the exceptional Thiemo Voigt at RISE-SICS in Stockholm, Sweden.

At the heart of my research lies the fascinating realm of Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS), where I delve into the intricacies of transiently-powered embedded systems. Never one to shy away from a challenge, I also immerse myself in the cutting-edge fields of embedded system security and network security, particularly honing my expertise in network forensics.

I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Department of Electronics, Information, and Bioengineering (DEIB), Politecnico di Milano, Italy, in February 2018, Under the guidance of the esteemed Associate Professor. Luca Mottola., my passion for technology and research blossomed. Prior to that, I was a Research Associate at the National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences' Systems and Network lab, under the mentorship of Associate Prof. Affan A.Syed.

I hold an MS in Computer Science and a BS in Telecommunications from the National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (FAST-NUCES), where I laid the foundation for my ever-evolving exploration of the digital world.
