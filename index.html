Assistant Professor, LUMS

Naveed Anwar Bhatti

I work on intermittent computing, embedded security, and cyber-physical systems.

My research focuses on dependable computing under constrained energy and sensing conditions, with an emphasis on batteryless systems, secure embedded intelligence, and deployable IoT systems for reliable real-world use. I am especially interested in building practical embedded platforms that remain robust under intermittent power and long-term field deployment constraints.

Previously ERCIM Alain Bensoussan Fellow at RISE. Ph.D. in Computer Science from Politecnico di Milano.
